Hot & Juicy Crawfish Restaurant

Hot & Juicy Crawfish restaurant is a seafood restaurant that has a unique blend of Louisiana style with hot Asian spicy taste to the food. I found this restaurant a couple years ago and just fell in love with the food. Especially the Hot & Juicy crawfish that is made with fresh/live crawfish. I was introduce to this place when I was visiting Las Vegas, Nevada and everytime I go back, I always find time to visit this restaurant each time.


Visit Hot & Juicy for more information about the restaurant and other locations.
